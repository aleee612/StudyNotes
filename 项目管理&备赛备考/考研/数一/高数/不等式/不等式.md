### 基础不等式
---
$$|a±b|≤|a|+|b|$$
$$||a|+|b||≤|a-b|$$
---
$$\sqrt{ab}=\dfrac{a+b}{2}\leq \sqrt{\dfrac{a^{2}+b^{2}}{2}}(a,b≥0)$$
$$\sqrt[3] {abc}\leq \dfrac{a+b+c}{3}\leq \sqrt{\dfrac{a^{2}+b^{2}+c^{2}}{3}}(a,b,c≥0)$$
---
$$0<a<x<b,0<c<y<d\Rightarrow \dfrac{c}{b} <\dfrac{y}{x} <\dfrac{d}{a}$$
---
$$\sin x<x<\tan x(0 <x <\dfrac{\pi }{2})$$
$$\sin x<x(x>0)$$
$$x<\tan x<\dfrac{4}{\pi }x(0 <x <\dfrac{\pi }{4})$$
$$\sin x>\dfrac{2}{\pi}x(0<x<\dfrac{\pi}{2})$$
$$\arctan x≤x≤\arcsin x(0≤x≤1)$$
